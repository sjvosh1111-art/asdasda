01. Brand Story

The Taste of Korea, Loved for Generations

Founded in 1967, Binggrae has been a symbol of Korean taste and happiness. From traditional dairy products to modern K-snacks, we deliver the essence of Korea to the world.

Core Message: "Binggrae is dedicated to creating the best customer value for the health and happiness of the people."

02. Signature Products

Icons of K-Food: Banana Milk & Melona

Banana Milk

A beloved Korean classic since 1974. With its soft banana flavor and unique bottle design, Banana Milk brings comfort and nostalgia to every sip.

Melona

The world-famous Korean ice cream bar known for its creamy texture and refreshing melon flavor. A true global K-dessert icon.
