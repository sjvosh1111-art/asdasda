K-Food Story & Positioning

Core copy: “Tradition + Innovation — a modern K-Food narrative”
  • Short editorial blocks explaining fermentation heritage, seasonal snacks, and how K-Food fits into global trends (health, convenience, fusion).
  • Include short explainer tiles (e.g., "Why K-Food now?", "Health & fermentation", "Street food → Global plates").

Recipes & Quick How-tos

Core copy: “Simple K-Food recipes using Binggrae favorites”
  • Recipe cards (photo, 3 steps) — example: Banana Milk Smoothie, Melona Parfait. Include short video thumbnails (demo assets fine).
  • Buttons for "Print", "Save", and "Share" to encourage social spread (OG tags & structured data recommended).

Where to Buy — Global Reach

Core copy: “Find K-Food near you — stores & online”
  • Interactive store finder (country selector → city → store list). Show which signature items (Banana Milk / Melona) are stocked.
  • Quick links to major international retailers when available (Costco, local convenience chains) and info about export expansion.

Campaigns & Social Proof

Core copy: “#TasteKorea — share your K-Food moment”
  • SNS feed embed (Instagram / TikTok hashtag stream), UGC gallery, contest CTA to drive sampling and recipe shares.
  • Localized campaign tiles per region (language, prize, partner retailer).
